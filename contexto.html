that are used to describe the highest or lowest degree of a quality, property, or characteristic. They are formed by adding the suffix "-est" to the adjective, except for some irregular forms.

  • Adjectives that end in "-e" or "-ie" become superlatives by adding "-st":

  • Big → biggest → the biggest

  • Adjectives that end in "-y" change the "-y" to "-i" and then add "-est":
  • Happy → happiest → the happiest

  • Adjectives that end in "-ful" or "-less" become superlatives by adding "-est":
  • Full → fullest → the fullest
